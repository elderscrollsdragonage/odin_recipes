Orange Cream Milkshake

Description

Our family loves milkshakes in the summer to cool off. The creamy orange flavor of this shake was a big hit.

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cupp milk
  • 4 scoops vanilla ice cream
  • 3 tablespoons frozen orange juice concentrate, thawed

Steps

  1. In a blender combine milk, ice cream and orange juice concentrate
  2. Blend until smooth
  3. Pour into glasses, garnish with an orange slice and serve
